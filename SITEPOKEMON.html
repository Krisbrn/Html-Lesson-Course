1. Bug Type
Bug-type Pokémon are agile and specialized in fast attacks. They are strong against Grass, Psychic, and Dark types but are vulnerable to Fire, Flying, and Rock. Examples: Caterpie, Scyther, Volcarona

2. Dark Type
Dark-type Pokémon are known for their deceptive abilities and mentally powerful attacks. They are strong against Psychic and Ghost types but are vulnerable to Fighting, Fairy, and other light types.
Examples: Umbreon, Darkrai, Sableye

3. Dragon Type
Dragon-type Pokémon are powerful, possessing great physical and mental strength. They are effective against many types, but have weaknesses to Dragon, Fairy, and Ice. They are among the most feared types in the Pokémon world.
Examples: Dragonite, Garchomp, Salamence

4. Electric Type
Electric-type Pokémon are fast and very effective in battle with powerful electric attacks. They are strong against Water and Flying types, but have a major weakness to Ground, which completely nullifies their electric moves.
Examples: Pikachu, Raichu, Zapdos

5. Fairy Type
Fairy-type Pokémon have magical and enchanting abilities. They are strong against Dragon, Dark, and Fighting types but weak against Poison and Steel. They are highly used for their resistance and offensive power.
Examples: Clefairy, Togekiss, Sylveon

6. Fighting Type
Fighting-type Pokémon excel in physical battles with powerful attacks. They are effective against Normal, Ice, Rock, Dark, and Steel types but have weaknesses to Psychic, Flying, and Fairy.
Examples: Machop, Lucario, Conkeldurr

7. Fire Type
Fire-type Pokémon are known for their destructive power and fast attacks. They are strong against Grass, Bug, Ice, and Steel types but are vulnerable to Water, Rock, and Ground.
Examples: Charmander, Arcanine, Entei

8. Flying Type
Flying-type Pokémon are fast and agile, with a great ability to evade attacks. They are strong against Bug, Grass, and Fighting types but weak against Electric, Ice, and Rock.
Examples: Pidgey, Staraptor, Lugia

9. Ghost Type
Ghost-type Pokémon are known for their supernatural abilities, such as deceiving and disorienting their opponents. They are strong against Psychic and Ghost types but have a weakness to Dark.
Examples: Gengar, Mimikyu, Drifloon

10. Grass Type
Grass-type Pokémon are closely linked to nature and are strong against Water, Ground, and Rock types. However, they are weak against Fire, Bug, Flying, Ice, and Poison.
Examples: Bulbasaur, Venusaur, Leafeon

11. Ground Type
Ground-type Pokémon are powerful in physical attacks and have impressive resistance. They are strong against Fire, Electric, Poison, Rock, and Steel types but weak against Water, Ice, Grass, and Water.
Examples: Groudon, Golem, Landorus

12. Ice Type
Ice-type Pokémon are known for their ability to deal significant damage and possess very powerful moves. However, they are extremely fragile, with weaknesses to Fire, Steel, Fighting, Rock, and Ice.
Examples: Articuno, Glaceon, Frostlass

13. Normal Type
Normal-type Pokémon are quite balanced, with good resistance and well-rounded stats. They don’t have clear advantages against other types but have weaknesses to Fighting types.
Examples: Pidgey, Snorlax, Eevee

14. Poison Type
Poison-type Pokémon specialize in weakening their opponents with toxic substances. They are strong against Fairy and Grass types but weak against Psychic and Ground.
Examples: Ekans, Muk, Gengar

15. Psychic Type
Psychic-type Pokémon have powerful mental abilities, capable of performing extremely effective attacks. They are strong against Fighting and Poison types but vulnerable to Ghost and Dark.
Examples: Mewtwo, Alakazam, Espeon

16. Rock Type
Rock-type Pokémon have immense defense, making them tough to defeat. They are strong against Flying, Fire, Bug, and Ice types but weak against Water, Grass, Fighting, Ground, and Steel.
Examples: Onix, Tyranitar, Geodude

17. Steel Type
Steel-type Pokémon are extremely resistant due to their solid defense. They are strong against Ice, Rock, and Fairy types but weak against Fire, Fighting, and other types with high resistance to physical attacks.
Examples: Steelix, Mawile, Scizor

18. Water Type
Water-type Pokémon are known for their versatility in battle. They are strong against Fire, Ground, and Rock types but weak against Electric and Grass. They are one of the most common and balanced types.
Examples: Squirtle, Gyarados, Vaporeon
